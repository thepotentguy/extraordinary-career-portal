Tour Guide

Extraordinary Adventures

Cape Town, South Africa

Full-time

Closing Date: 30th June 2024

Job Description:

We are seeking an enthusiastic and knowledgeable Tour Guide to join our team at Extraordinary Adventures. The ideal candidate will have a passion for history and culture, excellent communication skills, and the ability to engage with diverse groups of tourists.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead groups on tours of historical and cultural sites
  • Provide informative and engaging commentary
  • Ensure the safety and satisfaction of all tour participants
  • Assist with tour planning and coordination

Job Requirements:

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Knowledge of local history and culture
  • Experience in guiding tours and handling groups
  • Fluency in multiple languages is a plus

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary
  • Health insurance
  • Paid time off
  • Professional development opportunities

How to Apply:

Interested candidates can apply by sending their resume and a cover letter to careers@extraordinary.co.za.
